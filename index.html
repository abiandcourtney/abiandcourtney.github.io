About Us

Abi and Courtney met in England in early 2016 when Courtney worked for a boys' school in London. They quickly discovered a shared love of spending time outdoors, and even went on their first backpacking trip together as a third date! Since moving to California in the fall of 2017, they have had ample opportunities to enjoy roadtrips in the western US, hiking in the Sierras, and even scuba diving in Hawaii. They have also learned to mountain bike (slowly) and surf (pretty badly). In June of 2020, they got married in a local courthouse in California. They are so excited to finally celebrate their wedding with you all!

Wedding Ceremony

Semi-Formal
Our celebration will be somewhat less dressy than a typical british wedding - suits, dresses, and jumpsuits would all fit in well. Fascinators or hats are traditional british wedding attire and you are welcome to wear them. The wedding ceremony will be outside on a grassy lawn (weather permitting), so we recommend avoiding stilettos.

Everything Else

Casual
The rest of the weekend, wear whatever you feel comfortable in! Make sure to pack rain jackets or other rain-friendly attire.
